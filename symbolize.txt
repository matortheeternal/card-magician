we have some sequence of characters
at some point in the sequence, there may be a forward slash
when there is a forward slash, the character before and after the forward slash get merged together
merged characters can also be merged together, in some cases
when a character isn't recognized it is deleted

allowed characters:

/[WUBRGC0-9\|\/FLXYZ]/i
/[SEI]/i
/h/i

sequences:
phyrexian: /([wubrg]\/h)/i
generic or: /([0-9XYZ]\/[wubrgc])/i
half: /(\|[wubrgc])/i
large number: /([1-2][0-9]|30)/i
